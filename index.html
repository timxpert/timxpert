The Process

Wondering what the process looks like? Here’s how your project flows from first call to final delivery.

01

Book a Call

We jump on a quick call to discuss your product, target audience, and exactly what you want the video to achieve.

02

Project Brief

After the call, you’ll fill a short brief covering your goals, message, and any references. This keeps everything clear from the start.

03

Storyboard / Styleframes

With the script locked in, I design storyboard frames to visualize the direction, pacing, and style of the video for your approval.

04

Animation

Now, sit back and relax. This is where everything comes to life as I animate the visuals based on the approved storyboard.

05

Delivery & Feedback

Once the animation is ready, you’ll review the draft. I make the final tweaks and deliver the finished, ready-to-use video.
